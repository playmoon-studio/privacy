Account and Data Deletion

Playmoon Studio

This page explains how to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to playmoondev with the subject: "Account Deletion"
  2. In the email body, include the Google account email address used to sign in to the app along with the app name.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, all data associated with your account will be permanently removed.

Backups

Backup copies may remain in secure storage for up to 90 days before being automatically removed during the normal backup cycle.
