Account and Data Deletion

This app is developed by Playmoon Studio. This page explains how to request the deletion of your account and data.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to playmoondev@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion".
  2. In the email body, provide the Google account email address used to sign in to the app along with the app name.

Deleted data

When processing your request, we permanently remove all the data associated with your account.

Retained data

Backups

Backup copies are deleted during the normal backup cycle, within 90 days after deletion.
